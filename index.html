Activities

Explore our wide range of activities

Boat
Riding

Boat Riding lets you drift across sparkling waters, feel the breeze, and soak in stunning views all around. It’s a peaceful escape that blends relaxation with a touch of adventure.

Camping

Camping at Napoleon's is a one-of-a-kind experience. Unwind after a day of adventure, connect with family or friends, and fall asleep to the soothing sounds of the Sagana waterfalls—nature’s perfect therapy that will make you want to return again and again

Kayaking

Kayaking lets you glide across calm waters, surrounded by nature and fresh air. It’s a peaceful way to explore hidden corners and enjoy the moment.

Bike Riding

Bike Riding lets you cruise through scenic trails, feel the breeze, and enjoy the journey at your own pace. It’s a simple and fun way to explore and unwind.

Nature Trail

Nature Trail offers a thrilling adventure through the lush landscapes along the Sagana River. Feel the rush of excitement as you navigate scenic paths and discover the hidden gems of the riverside terrain.

Swimming

This is perfect for those looking to unwind and soak up the sun at Jangwani. Swim in the calm waters right in front of the camp—or for a real thrill, take the plunge from the tallest waterfall and feel the rush as you hit the sparkling river below!

Ziplining

Zip Line Adventure lets you fly above the treetops, feel the wind rush past, and experience pure thrill. It’s an unforgettable ride that will leave your heart racing

Full Rafting

Get ready for an adrenaline rush as you navigate the Sagana River’s thrilling rapids. Depending on the season, explore hidden caves, plunge from waterfalls, slide down natural rock formations, and float along scenic stretches for the ultimate adventure.

Team Building

Team Building turns fun into strategy! Take part in exciting games and challenges that test teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving—because together, we can achieve anything.

Enjoy a meal

Dining Experience is where great food meets great company. Share laughter, stories, and delicious meals—because at Napoleon’s Rafting Camp, every bite is part of the adventure!
